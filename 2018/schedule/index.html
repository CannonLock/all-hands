    OSG All-Hands Meeting 2018 – Schedule¶

    The Open Science Grid (OSG) All-Hands Meeting 2018 will be held March 19–22.

    High-Level Schedule¶

    Day Date Main Topics
    Monday March 19 FIFE, US-ATLAS, and US-CMS meetings; evening reception
    Tuesday March 20 General OSG topics; group dinner at Squatters Brewpub SLC
    Wednesday March 21 General OSG topics
    Thursday March 22 OSG training* (morning only); OSG Council meeting (all day)

    * There will be training sessions for users and site administrators.

    There may be a group ski trip organized for Friday, March 23; see the On Your Own page for more information.

    Detailed Agendas¶

    The detailed agenda is available on the Fermilab Indico system.

    The agenda for the US ATLAS Facilities Meeting is on their CERN Indico page.

    OSG All-Hands Meeting 2020 – Schedule¶

    The Open Science Grid (OSG) All-Hands Meeting 2020 will be held March 16–19.

    Special Focus Areas¶

    • Supporting researchers and their campuses
    • Science with GPUs and in the commercial cloud
    • Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML)

    High-Level Schedule¶

    Note: Each day includes light breakfast, lunch, plus snacks and beverages. The full agenda is available on the Fermilab Indico system.

    Monday (March 16)

    All day

    OSG and Multi-Institutional Projects

    E.g., IceCube, LIGO, DES, GlueX, CLAS12, DUNE, NOvA, FIFE, Science Gateways

    U.S. ATLAS

    U.S. CMS

    Evening

    Reception for all attendees (included in registration)
    Tuesday (March 17)

    Morning

    OSG on Campuses

    How researchers on campuses use OSG, including science stories and results from real projects

    Joint U.S. ATLAS & CMS

    Afternoon

    OSG on Campuses

    How OSG works, how we can help, reports from campuses who are working with OSG

    OSG Technology

    Reports and upcoming developments

    Evening

    Dinner for all attendees (included in registration)
    Wednesday (March 18)

    Morning (part 1)

    Tribute to David Swanson

    Remembrances, plus talks from recipients of the David Swanson Memorial Award

    Morning (part 2)

    Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and dHTC

    Afternoon (part 1)

    Cloud Integration and GPUs

    Afternoon (part 2)

    State of the OSG

    News and outlook
    Thursday (March 19)

    Morning

    Researcher Training

    Identifying and executing dHTC work that is appropriate for the OSG

    OSG Site Admin Training

    How to contribute resources to the OSG

    OSG Council Face-to-Face Meeting

    Early afternoon

    Boxed lunch for all attendees (included in registration)

    OSG All-Hands Meeting 2020 – Hotels¶

    The Open Science Grid (OSG) All-Hands Meeting 2020 will be held March 16–19 at the University of Oklahoma. The meeting itself will be in the Thurman J. White Forum Building (1704 Asp Ave).

    Lodging Options¶

    There is an on-campus student housing option, plus two nearby hotel blocks available.

    Cross Apartments (On Campus)¶

    • Student housing option just across the street from the conference venue
    • Go online to request a room
    • Conference rate: $30/night + taxes
    • Shared spaces: Each suite has 4 sleeping rooms, 2 bathrooms, and common areas

    La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham¶

    • Located about 3 miles from the conference venue
    • Go online, or call (888) 312-2399 and use “OSG All-Hands Meeting” to get the special rate
    • Special rate: $72/night + taxes
    • Rooms have two queen beds or one king bed
    • Check in time is 3 p.m., check out time is noon

    Fairfield Inn & Suites Norman¶

    • Located about 4 miles from the conference venue
    • Go online, or call (888) 236-2427 and use “OSG All-Hands Meeting” to get the special rate
    • Special rate: $84/night + taxes
    • Rooms have two queen beds or one king bed
    • Check in time is 3 p.m., check out time is noon
